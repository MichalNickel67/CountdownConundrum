How to Play

Get ready for the ultimate word challenge!

  • Click a button to start the game.
  • The timer will start automatically.
  • You’ll be shown a scrambled word — type your guess.
  • Click “Check” to submit your answer.
  • You win by guessing 5 words correctly!
