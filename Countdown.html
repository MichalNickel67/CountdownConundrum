Countdown Conundrum

Rules of the Game:

  • Click the Button for the Game that you want to play
  • The timer begins counting
  • You will have to guess 5 randomly shuffled words
  • The shuffled word will appear in the middle of the screen
  • Then you have to enter your Guess in the Textbox below
  • Click the Check button to check if your Guess is correct
  • You Win the game when you guess 5 words correctly
